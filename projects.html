FIFTH THIRD BANK CLIENT CHALLENGE

JAN 2019 - MAY 2019

Our team was tasked to better their inclusivity and diversity specifically within the Asian American community. Our team analyzed consumer banking trends and client-provided SQL data. By utilizing this information, we developed and presented an innovative solution for Fifth Third Bank.

FIRST YEAR INTEGRATED CORE

JAN 2019 - MAY 2019

Worked with Fifth Third bank in the Farmer School of Business First Year Integrated Core (FYIC) to create an industry disruptor Fifth Third Bank could implement.

The FYIC consists of four courses: Foundations of Business, Foundations of Business Communication, Creativity, Innovation and Entrepreneurship, and Introduction to Computational Thinking for Business.

These courses are specifically designed to build the skills needed for student success in the workplace.

ADVANCING WOMEN IN ENTREPRENEURSHIP POP UP SHOP

OCT 2018

Advancing Women in Entrepreneurship hosts a pop-up shop featuring female-founders once in the fall and once in the spring. Our goal is to bring light to businesses of all kinds to facilitate a supportive community to sell their products, tell students about their story, network with other female entrepreneurs, and gain brand exposure.

The Fall 2018 Pop Up Shop consisted of 10 female-ran vendors showcasing their services/products and stories in Miami's student center; over $200 in sales were made.
