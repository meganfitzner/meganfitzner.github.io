INTRODUCTION

Hello and welcome to a page that will tell you all that you need to know about me.

My name is Megan Fitzner and I am an incoming senior earning a marketing degree from Miami University's Farmer School of Business along with a minor in Emerging Technology in Business and Design from the College of Creative Arts.

I am passionate about helping others and my ultimate goal is to make a difference in this world. Through my numerous marketing courses, I have been able to better understand how marketing can affect those around me in a positive way. Some of my favorite courses that I have taken during my time at Miami University include Professional Communications for Business, Humanities and Technology, Digital Branding Analysis, and Building and Managing Strong Brands.

This website was developed in part for a coding course project, but in another part as a way for me to share who I am off paper. Not only am I a student; but I am a sister, daughter, and friend.
