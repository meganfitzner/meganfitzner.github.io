what I enjoy doing

BEING ACTIVE

Staying active while moving around opened the door for me to try out many different activities while I was growing up. Cheer, volleyball, softball, and others proved to be enjoyable for a while, but what I truly became passionate about was gymnastics and dance.

One of my favorite memories of senior year is the finale of our senior dance recital. It was such a special time to reminisce over all of the many years of competitions we participated in, the things I've learned how to do, and the impact my studio had on me.

Aside from organized activities, I also generally enjoy staying active in any way that I can. That typically comes in the form of being outside (walking, boating, hiking, kayaking, etc.) or going to the gym.
